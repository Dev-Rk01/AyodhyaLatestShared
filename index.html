Places of interest in Ayodhya

CHAITANYA TRAVELS

Ram Janmabhoomi

The birthplace of Lord Rama

The Supreme Personality Of Godhead. The most astonishing temple has undergone various transformations and has historical and religious importance.

Kanak Bhawan

dedicated to Lord Rama and his consort Sita

This temple is dedicated to Lord Rama and his consort Sita. The interior of the temple is adorned with beautiful idols of the deities.

Hanuman Garhi

A temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman,

it is one of the most visited places in Ayodhya. The temple is located on a mound and offers panoramic views of the city.

Swarg Dwar

Also called Heavenly Gate

It is believed to be the place from where Lord Rama returned to heaven. Pilgrims visit this spot during their visit to Ayodhya.
